Careers

Are you smart, passionate, talented and driven? Do you want to work with a growing company that will guarantee you the best experience and rewards?

If so, then MechFinder is looking for you. This new website is young, but innovative and we are looking for people who are ready to grow with us. We believe in making it easier for both mechanics and clients to meet and communicate. We are one of the fastest growing mobile mechanic networks in the world. The idea has a lot of potential and we believe that, with a talented team, we can make it even bigger. We think the solution we offer could be very beneficial to many people, therefore, we are working hard to build a company that will last. We need people who believe in this vision to team up with us so that we can take MechFinder to even greater heights. Does this sound like you? Then consider joining us in any of the following positions.

Sales Associates

We are looking for driven individuals who will help us to spread the word about just how special this new website is. Such people must be resilient, and they should have great communication skills. They should use any and every avenue at their disposal to promote our site and inform people about the benefits of using it. This can be achieved through social media marketing, through flyers and stickers on cars and other means. The sales associate will also visit a number of mechanics in the various areas. They should be able to convince the mechanics to sign up with MechFinder. They can outline all the benefits, such as better access to clients and the ability to work from home.

The sales associate should also help us to get advertisers for our website. Our website is still very young and there are a lot of advertisement spaces available. This is a great opportunity for any mechanic who wants to stand out. Furthermore, our advertisement prices are some of the lowest in the industry. Since we specifically deal with mechanics and their clients, the mechanic can be sure that they are getting the very best targeted marketing at a very reasonable price.

The Sales Associate at MechFinder should be able to see opportunities and possibilities in all the situations in which they are involved. They should be able to charm their way into the hearts of both users and advertisers. This is a job that you can be enthusiastic about, with competitive salary and benefits.

Software Developer

We are very interested in working with talented and innovative software developers. As a software developer at MechFinder, you will be developing tools and applications that will help our users to interact with each other. We project that our system will have a large number of users in the future, therefore we need an ambitious designer who is ready to drive the MechFinder platform so that it can best serve all of our users. This website has the potential to become one of the biggest sites of its kind on the internet. We need people who believe in this idea and who are talented, high achievers. We are also looking for people who are self-motivated and highly ambitious. Our software developers should be knowledgeable in one or more of these areas: Photoshop, Dreamweaver, JavaScript, Node JS and Mongo DB.

The Benefits of Working for Us

You will have exposure to a company that is fast-paced and determined to become the largest in the industry. We already have a team of very talented individuals, but we are always looking for more. The salaries and commissions that our employees earn are very competitive. The working environment is exciting and vibrant, and our team is made up of friendly people. We encourage our employees to be innovative and creative. In the end, you will help to build a website that will have a positive impact on the lives of numerous people. We are an equal opportunity employer and we do not discriminate on the basis of color, race or gender.

Do you believe that you have what it takes to work at MechFinder?
